# 中国商品期货量化研究  
# Quantitative Research on China Commodity Futures Market

面向中国商品期货市场的特征工程、信号构建与量化策略研究项目。  
A research project focused on feature engineering, signal construction, and quantitative strategy development for the China commodity futures market.

## 项目简介  
## Project Overview

本项目聚焦中国商品期货市场中的系统化交易研究，围绕因子/特征挖掘、交易信号生成、策略设计、回测评估与绩效分析展开，强调研究流程的清晰性、方法的可解释性以及策略开发的实用性。  
This project focuses on systematic trading research in the China commodity futures market, covering factor and feature exploration, trading signal generation, strategy design, backtesting evaluation, and performance analysis. It emphasizes a clear research workflow, methodological interpretability, and practical strategy development.

## 研究内容  
## Research Scope

- 商品期货特征工程与因子构建  
  Feature engineering and factor construction for commodity futures

- 量化交易信号设计与筛选  
  Quantitative trading signal design and selection

- 策略回测与绩效评估  
  Strategy backtesting and performance evaluation

- 风险控制与稳健性分析  
  Risk control and robustness analysis

- 系统化交易思路研究与迭代  
  Research and iteration of systematic trading methodologies

## 项目目标  
## Project Objective

通过构建高效特征与量化策略框架，探索适用于中国商品期货市场的系统化研究方法与交易思路。  
The objective of this project is to develop an efficient feature and quantitative strategy framework, and to explore systematic research methods and trading ideas suitable for the China commodity futures market.
